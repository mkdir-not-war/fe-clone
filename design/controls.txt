==== IN COMBAT ============================
* RT/LT - swap screens/characters
== on your turn ==
* LS - move
* X - prepare special attack
* Y - prepare ultimate attack
* B - prepare basic attack
* A - confirm attack, end your turn
== on enemy turn, during attack cinematic ==
* X - dodge (crush attacks)
* Y - jump (sweep attacks)
* B - counter (stab attacks)

==== OUT OF COMBAT ============================
* LS - move
* A - confirm
* B - cancel
* start - pause menu
